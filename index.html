Support

We are here to help! If you have any questions or need assistance, please feel free to reach out to our support team. Below are some common resources to assist you.

How do I reset my password?

Click the "Forgot Password" link on the login page and follow the instructions to reset your password.

How can I contact support?

You can contact our support team via the "Contact Us" form on our website or email us directly at payvaultsupport@gmail.com.

How do I cancel my subscription?

To cancel your subscription, visit the "Account Settings" page and click "Cancel Subscription" under the Billing section.
